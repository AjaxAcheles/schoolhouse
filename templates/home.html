Math

Mathematics is often disliked due to its tedious, time-consuming, and confusing nature. However, I believe in transforming students' perception of math by providing thorough explanations of concepts. This helps students understand the reasoning behind formulas, develop shortcuts, and make connections. I strive to create an engaging learning environment, encourage active participation, and foster a growing mind.

Computer Programming

Coding, or computer programming, is a means of communicating with computers through instructions written in code. It offers numerous possibilities, including website and app development, data processing, and more. In my coding classes, I teach students either Python, a widely used programming language, or Website Development, which combines multiple languages for creating functional websites. Students gain a solid foundation in coding concepts and develop practical skills through hands-on exercises and collaborative projects. Coding skills enhance logical thinking, analytical reasoning, and attention to detail, preparing students for various industries in the digital age.
